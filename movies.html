Synopsis

In a world where dreams become reality, a reluctant hero must confront their past to save the future. Archaeologist Dr. Elena Voss discovers fragments of an ancient device able to reshape memory and reality. Pursued by a shadowy organization, she teams with a rogue physicist and must choose what the world will remember.
