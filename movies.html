In a world where dreams become reality, a reluctant hero must confront their past to save the future...

When ancient artifacts begin appearing in modern-day cities, archaeologist Dr. Elena Voss discovers they're fragments of a device that can reshape reality. Pursued by a shadowy organization, she teams up with a rogue physicist to prevent the world from unraveling at the seams.
